MCC-REG-1 authority sources
---------------------------

The region definition and authority standard specifies Statistics Canada 2021
Dissemination Areas as the topology units and 2021 Economic Regions as broad
generation guardrails. canada-region-membership.csv and
canada-region-partition.geojson are derived from those sources. The original
Statistics Canada shapefiles are not redistributed. sources.lock.json records
the exact downloads, byte sizes, dates, licences, and SHA-256 hashes used to
generate the committed candidate layer.

Statistics Canada source information and Open Licence:

https://www150.statcan.gc.ca/n1/pub/92-160-g/92-160-g2021001-eng.htm
https://www.statcan.gc.ca/en/subjects/standard/sgc/2021/er-additionalinfo
